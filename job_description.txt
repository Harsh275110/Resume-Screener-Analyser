Data Science Manager

Job Description:
We are looking for an experienced Data Science Manager to lead our growing data science team. The ideal candidate will have a strong technical background in data science, machine learning, and analytics, combined with excellent leadership skills. You will be responsible for leading a team of data scientists, setting project priorities, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver high-impact data solutions for our business.

Responsibilities:
- Lead and mentor a team of data scientists and analysts
- Define and implement data science strategies aligned with company objectives
- Oversee the development of machine learning models and algorithms
- Collaborate with product, engineering, and business teams to identify opportunities for data-driven solutions
- Establish best practices for data science methodology, tools, and workflows
- Translate complex analytical findings into actionable business insights
- Manage project timelines, resources, and deliverables
- Stay current with the latest advancements in data science, machine learning, and AI

Requirements:
- Master's or PhD in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field
- 5+ years of hands-on experience in data science and machine learning
- 3+ years of experience managing data science teams
- Expert knowledge of Python, R, SQL, and data science libraries (scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch)
- Experience with big data technologies (Spark, Hadoop)
- Strong understanding of statistical analysis, experimental design, and causal inference
- Excellent communication skills and ability to translate technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders
- Proven track record of delivering data science projects that drive business value
- Experience with cloud computing platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience in our industry or with similar data challenges
- Knowledge of data visualization tools (Tableau, PowerBI)
- Experience with MLOps and deployment of models to production
- Background in deep learning or natural language processing
- Experience with agile development methodologies
- Contributions to open-source projects or research publications
- Understanding of data privacy regulations and ethical AI frameworks
